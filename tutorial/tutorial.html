Welcome to Pointer!

In this game, you are a wizard's apprentice trying to learn what a series of magic stones do.

The magic stones look like this:

You start with a score between -10 and +10, and using each stone will change your score somehow.

The stones are laid out in 2 paths. Once you choose a path, you must complete it by clicking on all the stones.

In order to win, your score after you've used all the stones must match the goal score displayed at the top of the screen.

Your start score is displayed on the left, and target on the right, like this:

The score will be updated after clicking on each stone. Pay attention to what happens!

It is important to give the game your full focus, and it must be played in fullscreen mode. Exiting fullscreen will cause the game to exit.

Good Luck!
